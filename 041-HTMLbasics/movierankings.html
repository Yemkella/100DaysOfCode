The Best Movies According to Jose

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Star Wars

I grew up watching all of the star wars films. I love these movies... except for the sequal trilogy. My favorite has to be The Empire Strikes Back.

Howl's Moving Castle

This is our favorite anime film to watch together as a family. It has beautiful animation and a magical story.

The Dark Knight

I am a big batman fan and Christopher Nolan created a masterpiece of a film.
